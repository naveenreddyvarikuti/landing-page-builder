Art in Every Frame

I’m Lena Cross — a fine-art wedding and portrait photographer living in the golden hills of Tuscany. My work is rooted in documentary storytelling, crafting luminous, unposed images that honor authenticity and artistry. I seek to capture the subtle poetry of your story: laughter in the olive grove, vows whispered under cypress trees, light tracing the contours of a new beginning. Every moment deserves reverence. Let’s create images you’ll treasure for a lifetime.
